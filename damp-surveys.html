Independent Damp Surveys

Our independent damp surveys identify excessive moisture, potential damp problems, and any related wood-boring insects or fungal issues.

Types of Damp Surveys We Offer:

  • Damp & Timber Surveys: Identifies damp issues, timber decay, and fungal infestations.
  • Damp & Mould Surveys: Assesses moisture conditions contributing to mould growth.
  • Damp & Structural Moisture Investigations: In-depth analysis of structural moisture penetration.

These surveys are particularly useful for homebuyers, landlords, and property managers.
