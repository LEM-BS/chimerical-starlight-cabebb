Our Professional Surveying Services

We offer a wide range of property surveying services, ensuring you have expert guidance for your property investments.

  • RICS Level 1 Home Survey - A basic condition report suitable for newer properties.
  • RICS Level 2 Home Survey - A mid-level report providing a detailed assessment of property condition.
  • RICS Level 3 Home Survey - A comprehensive structural survey for older or complex properties.
  • Independent Damp Surveys - Expert analysis of moisture, damp, and potential structural damage.
  • Fire Safety Assessments - Evaluating fire risk and passive fire protection in buildings.
  • Commercial Property Surveys - Detailed reports for business properties and investment decisions.
  • Measured Building Surveys - Accurate property measurements for development, planning, or design.
  • Ventilation Assessments - Residential ventilation inspections to ensure compliance with Building Regulations Part F.

Each service is tailored to provide the best insight for your needs. Contact us today for professional, reliable surveying services.
